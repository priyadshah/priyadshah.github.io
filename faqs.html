Where do you tutor?
We offer tutoring services in Durham, Cary, Apex, and Raleigh.
Do you offer any referral discounts?
Yes! For every student that you refer that purchases a monthly tutoring package, you will receive a 5% discount off your next month of tutoring sessions.
Do you offer tutoring online?
Yes! We offer tutoring via Google Hangout and Skype!
Is tutoring private or in a group setting?
While each tutoring session is individual, group tutoring is available upon request. Classes in groups are also offered during the summer.
How do you track student progress?
Topics covered and student progress will be tracked after each session. Student progress reports are available upon request.
